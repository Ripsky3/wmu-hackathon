What is the Western Michigan University Hackathon?

Hack WMU is a forthcoming hackathon in West Michigan, hosted at Western Michigan University by the Google Developer Student Clubs chapter. The event is designed to offer students a creative environment where they can tackle challenges and devise real-world solutions using technology.
